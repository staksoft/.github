## 🚀 About Us

**Staksoft** is a premier software development company specializing in building secure, scalable, and intelligent digital products. We solve complex engineering problems across high-demand and regulated industries, delivering top-tier solutions in **HealthTech, E-Commerce, IoT, AI, SaaS, and Mobile App Development**. 

Our engineering ethos focuses on robust microservices architecture, HIPAA-compliant systems, and privacy-first AI integration to ensure your digital transformation is both future-proof and resilient.

## 🛠️ Our Core Services

We provide end-to-end software engineering and product development services:

- 🏥 **HealthTech & IoT:** HIPAA-compliant systems, medical device integrations, PulseOx, and real-time patient monitoring dashboards.
- 🛒 **E-Commerce Engineering:** High-performance storefronts, headless commerce, Magento, and Shopify development.
- 📱 **Mobile App Development:** Cross-platform native performance apps with Flutter, featuring custom camera/OCR and offline-first capabilities.
- ⚙️ **Microservices & APIs:** Robust backend architecture using Node.js, NestJS, Golang, gRPC, and event-driven distributed systems.
- ☁️ **SaaS Product Development:** Multi-tenant scalable cloud platforms, subscription engines, and intuitive dashboards.
- 🚀 **Cloud & DevOps:** Secure, automated deployments using Docker, Kubernetes, AWS/GCP, CI/CD, and serverless architectures.
